# Privacy Policy for Mixby

Last updated: November 2025  
Contact: cau.snsn@gmail.com

## Introduction
Mixby (“the App”) is developed to provide cocktail recommendations, recipe browsing, and user preference personalization.  
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

This Privacy Policy describes how the App manages, stores, and interacts with user data.  
Mixby does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

---

## Information We Do Not Collect
Mixby does **not** collect or process any of the following:

- Name, email address, phone number  
- Location data  
- Contact information  
- Financial or payment information  
- Health or sensitive information  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Device identifiers  
- Usage analytics or behavior tracking  

The App operates fully **on-device**, meaning no user data is uploaded to external servers.

---

## Local Data Storage
The App may store the following information **locally on the device only**:

- Favorite cocktails
- User-generated notes
- Personal preference settings  
- Locally cached images or assets  

This data is stored exclusively on the user's device and is never transmitted to any remote server.

You may delete the App at any time to remove all locally stored data.

---

## Third-Party Services
The App does **not** integrate with third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services.

No third-party vendor receives or processes user data from this App.

---

## Network Communication
The App communicates only with Mixby’s internal recommendation API to retrieve cocktail data.  
No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

All communication is limited to:

- Fetching cocktail recipes  
- Fetching recommendation data  

No user profile or personal data is transmitted.

---

## Children’s Privacy
Mixby does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.  
The App is safe for general use.

The revised version will always be published at the same URL.
